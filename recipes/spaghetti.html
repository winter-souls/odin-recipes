Spaghetti

Description

Ingredients

  • one (1) bag of spaghetti noodles
  • water
  • olive oil
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • preferred spaghetti sauce
  • minced garlic
  • preferred seasonings (suggested: salt, pepper, onion powder, italian seasoning)
  • optional: parmesan cheese,

Steps

  1. Bring large pot of water to boiling
  2. Stir in a small lump of salt and a dash of olive oil (prevents sticking)
  3. Add spaghetti noodles to boiling water
  4. While waiting for noodles to boil, put skillet on medium heat
  5. Add minced garlic to skillet, followed by ground beef
  6. Season ground beef to taste and cook until no longer pink
  7. Optional: once ground beef is cooked, strain grease into grease jar and return to skillet
  8. Add preferred spaghetti sauce. Let meat finish cooking in the sauce
  9. Once noodles are done boiling, strain water and return to pot
  10. Mix meat sauce into noodles
  11. Serve and enjoy!
  12. Optional step: garnish each bowl with parmesan cheese
