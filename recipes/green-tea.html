Green Tea

Description

Ingredients

  • one (1) green tea bag
  • one lemon
  • honey

Steps

  1. Use preferred method to heat a glass of water
  2. Let tea bag steep in the water for 3 minutes
  3. While tea steeps, chop lemon into 8ths
  4. Keep 1/8th out and store the rest in a ziploc bag in the fridge for later
  5. Stir in honey to taste (recommended 1 teaspoon)
  6. Carefully squeeze the juice from 1/8th lemon into tea
  7. Stir
  8. Enjoy!
